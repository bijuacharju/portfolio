RESEARCHED WEBSITES

Personal Website of Devon Stank

The website of Devon Stank is simply marvelous. The way he used the suitable GIF (Animated Image) file at the background of his hero section of website and the placement of text over background at perfect place makes his website look attractive. The way he designed and maintained every component is awesome. While I was looking into his website, I was inspired with his design. So, I decided to use the text over background over the background. I introduced myself in the hero section with my own image as a background. I also changed the font color of my name to emphasize it.

Official Website of Microsoft

While I was surfing over internet, I went into the official website of Microsoft Corporation. In their website, different product produced by Microsoft is listed in the form of icons and some text below each product. The design was so impressive that it caught my eye particularly in that portion. The way they presented their product was really amazing. It was designed perfectly balancing every components of website. From this website of Microsoft, I was inspired to create the design of contact ribbon. My contact ribbon includes name of way of contact i.e. address, e-mail, contact number and website. It also contains suitable icon above each component and details below each component.

Personal Website of Er. Prakash Koirala

I was inspired to using slider in the skills portion of cv page from the website of Er. Prakash Koirala. He represented his skill level in the form of slider. The slider he used was bulky but was made to be completely blend with theme of the website. But in my website, I have kept a lot of free space, so I made my website a bit thinner. Also, I changed the color of slider to match it with the color of title to draw more attention of visitor towards the slider.

Official Website of Google Chrome

When I went into the official website of google chrome, I saw the standard layout of arranging text and image side by side. The way components managed in the website of google chrome was amazing. The text was made to position at the horizontally center with image. I was inspired with the design, so I maintained the similar layout in the home page of my website. My layout has the title at the top of the division and text aligned horizontally centered with my own image.

Personal Website of Robby Leonardi

The website of Robby Leonardi was really attractive. The footer section he designed was amazing. The way he kept the proportion of footer as per the content of website caught my eye. From this website I was inspired to make a big footer where I could provide contact information and provide my links of social media. At the footer, I created three sets of information which are contact information, useful links of websites and social media icons which redirects the user to my social media profile.
