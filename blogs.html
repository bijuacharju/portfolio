IMPACT OF TECHNOLOGY IN DAILY LIFE.

Technology is a branch of knowledge concerned with applied sciences. It is also regarded as the equipment, machinery and application developed from scientific knowledge for practical purposes. In this modern era, technology has dramatically impacted in the daily life of the humans. Nowadays, due to advancement in technology, we are surrounded by it no matter wherever we go in this world. This vast world is now regarded as the Global Village due to it.

We use technology and its product for daily life purposes. We are so addicted to technological gadgets that we can’t even imagine our daily life with comfort without it. We use computer, smartphone and internet in our daily life which is also the gift of technology. In early days, people use to send hand – written mail and it use to take days and even months to reach the addressed person. But technology has made it possible within seconds. We can use e-mail fax, SMS and other services to send mail to even cheaper cost.

Technology has changed our way of life. Using communication devices like mobile phone, we can communicate to our loved ones from one side to another side of the world. Using technological device like computer, even most complex calculation can be solved in an instant. We can see the use of technology in every field like agriculture, business, education, health care and lot more.

Using modern equipment and hybrid plants farmers can grow more foods within very short amount of time. This has let farmers to grow their economic status and finally change their way of living. Nowadays, with the use of internet, students can attend their classes through computers. They don’t have to travel an hour – long distance to get education which is the benefit of e-learning. We can get any kind of information regardless where we are with the use of technology.

Technological advancement has affected the business sector dramatically. Earlier days, people had to go to market and search for hours to get the stuff they needed. But in this technological era, people can go online to search the goods they need and order it. There are many e-commerce companies like Daraz, Amazon, etc. who sell different stuffs online.

In day to day life, technology is also widely being used int health sector. With the use of advanced technology, doctors can detect many different types of diseases in patient and treat them before it causes any serous damage to patient health. Due to this, many lives are being saved can return to their normal daily life.

The use of smartphones and artificial intelligence has increased a lot in last few decades. In early days, people who can’t read and write were regarded as illiterate people. But nowadays, people who can’t use devices like computer, smartphone and send e-mail are regarded as illiterate people. Smartphone is such a device that has almost all the functions of computer and is made within the size of palm. It can be used for various purposes. It can be used for telecommunication and talk to others at distant places. It can also be used to send and receive electronic mail. It has integrated features like calculator, note keeping, camera, radio, etc. People don’t have to invest their money on different devices, now, they can simply but one smartphone and get all the features at once. It is also the source of education, information and entertainment.

Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in our daily life has made our life much easier. AI is used in many software like Siri of apple, Cortana of windows, maps of google, etc. Voice command application like Siri and Cortana uses our voice as input and take actions as we instructed. Maps of google is also the product of AI. It helps to know where we are, how many ways are possible to reach our destination, shortest route to destination, if there is traffic on way and lot more. Using AI integrated software has made our life simpler easier and comfortable.

People can read news on their own phone and computer which made it possible to get updates on current scenario of the world. Technology has advanced so much that nowadays concepts of smart home is being one of the hot topics in the world of technology. With this technology, a person can watch over his home without being present at home. A person can control his house completely with his smartphone.

When I look at myself, I can see that I am completely confined by technology. My day to day activities are completely dependent within technological devices. I use technological device like computer for various purposes like education, research, gaming, entertainment, communication and lot more. As I am IT student, even our whole teaching system is dependent on technology. Now, technology is most vital part of my career. I interact many technological devices, equipments and applications in my day to day life.

I am highly interested in technology from a long time. That is why, I am persuading my dream and career in the field of Information Technology. Technology has made my life much easier and more comfortable. As I stay till midnight for various reasons, it gets hard for me to wake up early in the morning. In that situation the alarm of my phone is always there to wake me up which is also the product o technology. There are consequences of technology too, but we need to move forward towards development tackling and solving those problems. Also, my life and my career are completely dependent on technology.
